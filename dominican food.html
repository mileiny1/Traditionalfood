    Stewed Chicken

    Ingredients:

    • Chicken, 2 pounds
    • 1 pinch of oregano
    • 1 red onion
    • 1 red bell pepper
    • 1 green bell pepper
    • 9 garlic cloves
    • A few cilantro leaves
    • A few parsley leaves
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • 1 teaspoon of regular white sugar
    • 2 cups of water
    • 1 packet of culantro y achiote seasoning (optional)
    • 2 tablespoons sazon completo
    • 4 tomatoes cut into 4 quarters
    • 1 cup tomato sauce
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • To wash: 1/2 cup vinegar and enough water to submerge the chicken in

    Instructions:

    1. Chop the chicken into smaller pieces.
    2. Wash the chicken using vinegar and water.
    3. Blend half of the red onion, bell peppers, garlic cloves, oregano, and cilantro. Smash the other half of the garlic with salt.
    4. Season chicken with blended veggies, Sazon completo, oregano, and garlic paste. Season with salt and pepper.
    5. Heat oil in a pot, add sugar, and wait for it to turn dark golden. Add chicken and cook until browned.
    6. Add the remaining veggies, tomatoes, sazon with cilantro y achiote, and water. Cover and cook for 15 minutes.
    7. Add tomato sauce, stir, and cook until the chicken falls apart.

    Rice

    Ingredients:

    • 5 tablespoons oil
    • Salt, to taste
    • 6 cups water
    • 4 cups jasmine rice

    Instructions:

    1. Heat oil in a pot, add salt, and stir in the rice.
    2. Toast the rice, then add water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook for 20 minutes. Add water if needed.

    Beans

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cans of beans (pinto, kidney)
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1/2 red onion, sliced
    • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
    • 1/2 green bell pepper, sliced
    • A handful of cilantro
    • 4 cloves of garlic
    • Half of a squash (pumpkin, kabocha, or acorn) cut into cubes
    • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
    • 1 packet culantro y achiote seasoning
    • Salt, to taste
    • Pepper, to taste

    Instructions:

    1. Smash garlic with salt in a pestle and mortar.
    2. Heat olive oil, add garlic paste, veggies, and cilantro, and cook for about 5 minutes.
    3. Add tomato paste, culantro y achiote, beans, and one can of water. Cover and cook for 10-15 minutes.
    4. Blend or mash beans for desired consistency.
