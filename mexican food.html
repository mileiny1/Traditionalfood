Enchiladas Recipe

Ingredients

  • Guajillo peppers (seeds removed)
  • Ancho peppers (seeds removed)
  • Garlic cloves (chopped)
  • Mexican oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded beef, pork, or chicken (optional)
  • Fresh cheese (crumbled)
  • White onion (finely chopped)
  • Vegetable oil

Instructions

Follow these steps to prepare your enchiladas:

  1. Prepare the chili sauce by blending guajillo and ancho peppers with chopped garlic, Mexican oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry corn tortillas lightly to make them pliable.
  3. Dip each tortilla into the chili sauce, then fill with shredded meat (if using) and fresh cheese.
  4. Roll up the tortillas and place them in a baking dish.
  5. Top with additional chili sauce and chopped white onion.
  6. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20 minutes, or until heated through and cheese is melted.
